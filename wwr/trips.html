Popular trips

The Stroll

Ready to enjoy rapids and nature on the same trip? One full week experiencing the mountains and slot canyons and exploring the wilderness, traveling light rapids along the way. A 40 mile stretch of wonder.

The Get-Away

Not a rapids waterbug? Need a relaxing week in nature with amazing views and fresh air? This adventure includes a variety of camping locations, a nearby lake, miles of walking trails to mountain top views and nearby rivers, and nature all to yourself.

The Bear

An adventure only for the bravest; you'll want to share this adventure with your friends. A 3-day trip through 28 miles of monster rapids, with spectacular vistas and the occasional black bear, this is sure to be an unforgettable adventure!
